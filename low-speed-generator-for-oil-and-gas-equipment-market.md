<p><ul><li>Synchronous Generator</li><li>Asynchronous Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the low-speed generator market for oil and gas equipment, two primary types are synchronous and asynchronous generators. Synchronous generators operate at a constant speed and maintain a fixed phase relationship with the grid, making them suitable for stable power generation in remote locations. Asynchronous generators, or induction generators, can vary in speed and are often used in variable load applications. Both types cater to the industry's need for reliable energy solutions, optimizing efficiency and operational performance in challenging environments.</p></p>
