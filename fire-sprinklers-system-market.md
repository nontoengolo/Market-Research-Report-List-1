<p><p>The fire sprinkler system market features several key types: </p><p>1. **Wet Sprinkler System**: This system keeps water in the pipes at all times, providing immediate response to fire detection.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Dry Sprinkler System**: In this system, pipes are filled with pressurized air, making it suitable for environments prone to freezing, as water is stored in a separate tank.</p><p>3. **Deluge Sprinkler System**: Designed for high-hazard areas, this system releases water from all open sprinkler heads simultaneously when activated, offering rapid suppression of large fires.</p></p>
