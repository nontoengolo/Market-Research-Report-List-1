<p><p>The Form Fill and Sealing (FFS) equipment market comprises two primary types: Vertical Form Fill Sealing (VFFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Sealing (HFFS). VFFS equipment vertically forms bags from flat film, fills them with product, and seals them, making it ideal for products like powders and granules. In contrast, HFFS operates horizontally, forming and sealing bags for items such as snacks and pre-packaged goods. Each type caters to specific packaging needs and industry applications, enhancing efficiency and production rates.</p></p>
