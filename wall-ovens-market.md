Double wall ovens provide two separate cooking compartments, allowing for cooking multiple dishes simultaneously or at different temperatures, enhancing efficiency. Combined wall ovens integrate both oven and microwave functionalities, providing versatility in cooking methods while saving space. Each type caters to different consumer needs and kitchen layouts.</p></p>
