<p><ul><li>Refracting telescope</li><li>Reflector Telescope</li><li>Catadioptric telescope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical telescope market is categorized into three primary types: refracting telescopes use lenses to gather and focus light, offering sharp images ideal for planetary observation. Reflector telescopes utilize mirrors to reflect light, providing wider views and higher magnification, making them popular for deep-sky observation. Catadioptric telescopes combine lenses and mirrors, resulting in compact designs with versatile performance for both terrestrial and astronomical observations. Each type caters to different user needs, from amateur astronomers to professional research applications.</p></p>
