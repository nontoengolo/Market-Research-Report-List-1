<p><p>The Optical Character Recognition (OCR) market comprises various types, including desktop-based, mobile-based, and cloud-based OCR solutions. Desktop-based OCR is installed on local machines, offering powerful processing capabilities for document digitization. Mobile-based OCR utilizes smartphone applications for on-the-go text recognition, ideal for quick scans. Cloud-based OCR leverages remote servers, enabling scalable and collaborative document processing across devices. Additionally, other OCR solutions may include embedded systems or specialized software integrated into various applications, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
