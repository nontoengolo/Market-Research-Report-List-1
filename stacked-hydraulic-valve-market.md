<p><p>The stacked hydraulic valve market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Control Valve**: Regulates system pressure to prevent overload, ensuring safe operation in hydraulic systems.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Flow Control Valve**: Manages the flow rate of hydraulic fluid, allowing precise control over the speed and movement of hydraulic actuators.</p><p>3. **Check Valve**: Prevents backflow within hydraulic circuits, ensuring fluid flows in one direction and maintaining system integrity.</p><p>These valves are essential for efficient hydraulic system performance, enhancing reliability and control in various applications.</p></p>
