<p><p>The microporous film market features two primary types based on stretching techniques: unidirectional and biaxial stretching. Unidirectional stretching involves extending the film in one direction, enhancing its strength and barrier properties, making it suitable for specific applications like packaging and insulation. In contrast, biaxial stretching stretches the film in two directions, resulting in improved durability and flexibility, which broadens its applicability in industries such as medical, automotive, and construction. Both types cater to diverse performance requirements across various sectors.</p></p>
