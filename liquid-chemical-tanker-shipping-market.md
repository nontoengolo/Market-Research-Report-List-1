<p><p>The liquid chemical tanker shipping market is categorized into three types. Inland chemical tankers operate on inland waterways, transporting chemicals between ports along rivers and lakes. Coastal chemical tankers serve regional routes, moving chemicals between nearby coastal ports, often within a country's borders. Deep-sea chemical tankers cover extensive international routes, transporting large quantities of chemicals across oceans. Each type plays a crucial role in the logistics and distribution of various liquid chemicals globally, catering to specific shipping needs and challenges.</p></p>
