<p><p>Melt processable fluoropolymers tubing is categorized into various types, each with distinct properties and applications. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) offers excellent chemical resistance and transparency. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) is known for its high strength and thermal stability. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) provides exceptional temperature resistance and flexibility. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) is recognized for its mechanical strength and chemical resistance. ECTFE (Ethylene Chlorotrifluoroethylene) combines good mechanical properties with chemical resistance, while others may include specialty fluoropolymers catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
