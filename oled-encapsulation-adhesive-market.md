Pressure-sensitive encapsulating adhesives offer easy application and repositioning, ideal for flexible and complex surfaces. Other types may include UV-curing and thermal-curing adhesives, catering to diverse requirements in OLED manufacturing. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the longevity and performance of OLED devices.</p></p>
