<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Air Sampler market is divided into two main types: active and passive samplers. Active samplers use a mechanical pump to draw air through a filter or adsorbent material, collecting airborne contaminants for analysis. They are often used for precise measurements in various environments. In contrast, passive samplers rely on natural air flow to capture pollutants, making them simpler and more cost-effective. While active samplers provide real-time data, passive samplers are ideal for long-term monitoring and remote locations.</p></p>
