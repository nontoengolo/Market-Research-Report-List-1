<p><strong>The ELISA Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers</li><li>Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ELISA analyzers market can be categorized into two main types: optical filter ELISA analyzers and optical grating ELISA analyzers. Optical filter analyzers utilize specific wavelength filters to isolate and measure the absorbance of samples, offering high sensitivity and straightforward operation. In contrast, optical grating analyzers employ diffraction gratings to separate light into its component wavelengths, providing enhanced flexibility and accuracy for complex analyses. Both types are essential for various applications in clinical diagnostics and research settings.</p></p>
