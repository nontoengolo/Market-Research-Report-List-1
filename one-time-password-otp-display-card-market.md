<p><strong>The One Time Password (OTP) Display Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTP Magnetic Card</li><li>OTP Chip Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The One Time Password (OTP) display card market comprises two main types: OTP Magnetic Cards and OTP Chip Cards. OTP Magnetic Cards utilize a magnetic strip to generate temporary, unique passwords for secure transactions, often requiring swiping for access. In contrast, OTP Chip Cards are equipped with embedded microchips that securely store and generate OTPs, providing enhanced security with features like encryption. Both types are essential for multi-factor authentication, enhancing security across various digital platforms and reducing fraud risks.</p></p>
