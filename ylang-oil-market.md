<p><p>Ylang oil is categorized into several types based on the distillation process and quality. Ylang-Ylang Extra is the highest quality, with a sweet, floral aroma. Ylang-Ylang Complete blends all fractions and provides a balanced scent. Each type serves distinct uses in perfumery, aromatherapy, and cosmetics, catering to various consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
