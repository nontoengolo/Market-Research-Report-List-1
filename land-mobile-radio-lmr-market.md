<p><p>Land Mobile Radio (LMR) encompasses communication systems primarily used for public safety, transportation, and industrial applications. The market segments include VHF (40MHz - 174MHz), ideal for long-range communication in rural areas; UHF (200MHz - 512MHz), favored for urban settings due to its ability to penetrate structures; and SHF (700MHz - 1000MHz), increasingly utilized for advanced applications like broadband data. Each spectrum band serves specific operational needs, balancing range, signal clarity, and capacity for diverse user requirements.</p></p>
