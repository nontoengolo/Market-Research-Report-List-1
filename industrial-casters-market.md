<p><p>The industrial casters market is primarily divided into two main types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature a rotating wheel design that allows for easy maneuverability in multiple directions, making them ideal for handling complex layouts and tight spaces. Rigid casters, on the other hand, have a fixed wheel orientation, providing stability and straight-line movement, which is beneficial for heavy loads and long-distance transport. Together, these casters enhance operational efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
