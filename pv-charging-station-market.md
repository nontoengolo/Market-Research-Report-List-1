<p><p>The PV Charging Station market is divided into two main types: On-Grid and Off-Grid PV Power Stations. On-Grid PV Power Stations are connected to the main electricity grid, allowing for the sale of excess power back to the grid and providing stable energy supply. In contrast, Off-Grid PV Power Stations operate independently, utilizing battery storage for energy use, ideal for remote locations without grid access. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
