<p><p>The computed tomography (CT) market is categorized into low, medium, and high slice CT scanners based on the number of slices they capture per rotation. Low slice CT scanners, typically offering 1-4 slices, are suitable for basic imaging tasks. Medium slice scanners (8-16 slices) provide enhanced detail for routine diagnostics, while high slice CT scanners (32 slices and above) enable rapid, high-resolution imaging for complex conditions, making them ideal for advanced applications like cardiac and brain scans.</p></p>
