<p><p>The fixed-focus lens market comprises three key types: fixed iris, manual iris, and auto iris lenses. Fixed iris lenses have a permanently set aperture, providing consistent exposure but limited versatility. Manual iris lenses allow users to adjust the aperture for varying lighting conditions, offering more control over depth of field and exposure. Auto iris lenses automatically adjust the aperture in response to changing light levels, ensuring optimal image quality and exposure without manual intervention, making them ideal for dynamic environments.</p></p>
