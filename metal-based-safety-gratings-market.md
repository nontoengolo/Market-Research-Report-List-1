<p><p>The metal-based safety gratings market includes various types primarily categorized into flat, serrated, and press-locked steel bar gratings. Flat type gratings offer a smooth surface ideal for pedestrian applications, while serrated type gratings feature a notched surface for enhanced slip resistance, making them suitable for wet or oily environments. Press-locked steel bar gratings, created through a locking mechanism that holds bars securely together, provide a sturdy and durable solution for heavy loads, commonly used in industrial settings.</p></p>
