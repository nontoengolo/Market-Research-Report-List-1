<p><p>Saliva collection kits are essential tools for various applications, including medical diagnostics and research. These kits come in two primary types: room temperature kits and low-temperature kits. Room temperature kits are designed for ease of use and convenience, allowing samples to be stored without refrigeration, which is ideal for rapid testing and deployment in diverse environments. In contrast, low-temperature kits require refrigeration to preserve sample integrity, making them suitable for long-term storage and sensitive analyses that demand strict temperature control.</p></p>
