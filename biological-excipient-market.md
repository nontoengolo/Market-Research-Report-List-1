<p><p>The biological excipient market is categorized into two main types: oral and topical. Oral excipients are substances used in the formulation of oral dosage forms, such as tablets and capsules, to enhance the drug's stability, solubility, or absorption. Topical excipients are utilized in creams, ointments, and gels for applications on the skin or mucosal surfaces, providing drug hydration, penetration enhancement, or controlled release. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the efficacy and safety of biological products.</p></p>
