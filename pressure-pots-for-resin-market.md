<p><ul><li>Slow Pressure Tank</li><li>Quick Pressure Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure pots for resin play a crucial role in eliminating air bubbles during the casting process. Slow pressure tanks maintain a lower pressure over a longer period, allowing for gradual resin curing, which minimizes voids and defects. In contrast, quick pressure tanks operate at higher pressures for shorter durations, accelerating curing times while still reducing bubbles effectively. Both types cater to different project needs, with slow tanks favoring intricate applications and quick tanks benefiting those requiring faster turnaround without compromising quality.</p></p>
