<p><strong>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pressure CPAP Device</li><li>Auto Adjusting CPAP Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices market is primarily divided into two types: Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices and Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices. Fixed Pressure CPAP devices deliver a constant airflow at a predetermined pressure level, ensuring a steady supply of air to keep the airway open during sleep. In contrast, Auto Adjusting CPAP devices automatically adjust the pressure throughout the night based on the user's needs, providing a personalized and often more comfortable treatment experience for managing sleep apnea.</p></p>
