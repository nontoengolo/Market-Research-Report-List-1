<p><p>Micro Machining Systems are categorized based on the number of axes they operate on. Systems with less than 3 axes are typically used for simple, 2D operations. 3-axis machines add depth, allowing for complex shapes in one setup. 4-axis systems introduce rotation, enabling more intricate designs. 5-axis machines provide advanced flexibility and precision, accommodating complex geometries. Systems with more than 5 axes offer superior versatility, suited for high-end applications in industries like aerospace and medical, enhancing productivity and accuracy.</p></p>
