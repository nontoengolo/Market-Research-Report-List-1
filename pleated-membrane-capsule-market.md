<p><p>The Pleated Membrane Capsule Market encompasses various types of filtration membranes, each serving specific needs. PP (Polypropylene) filters are known for their chemical resistance and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for water filtration. PES (Polyethersulfone) filters offer high flow rates and low protein binding, suitable for biopharmaceutical applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) filters provide excellent hydrophobic properties, ideal for gaseous processes. Nylon filters are versatile and provide good mechanical strength. The "Others" category includes specialized membranes catering to niche applications, enhancing market diversity.</p></p>
