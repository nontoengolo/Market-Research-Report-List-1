<p><ul><li>One-finger Touch Measurement</li><li>Two-finger Pressure Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG smartwatch market features two primary measurement types: one-finger touch measurement and two-finger pressure measurement. One-finger touch measurement allows users to initiate an ECG reading by placing one finger on the device, streamlining the process for quick assessments. In contrast, two-finger pressure measurement requires users to place two fingers on designated sensors, enhancing accuracy by capturing more comprehensive cardiac data. Both types cater to different user preferences, contributing to the growing demand for health-monitoring wearable technology.</p></p>
