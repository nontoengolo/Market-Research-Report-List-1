<p><strong>The Anesthesia Video Laryngoscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channelled Video Laryngoscopes</li><li>Un-Channelled Video Laryngoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anesthesia video laryngoscope market is segmented into channelled and un-channelled video laryngoscopes. Channelled video laryngoscopes feature a built-in channel that allows for the passage of endotracheal tubes, facilitating easier intubation, especially in difficult airway scenarios. In contrast, un-channelled video laryngoscopes have a simpler design without a dedicated channel, offering better visualization of the vocal cords but requiring manual tube handling. Both types enhance the efficacy and safety of airway management in anesthesia practices.</p></p>
