<p><p>The 3D Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) market is primarily divided into two types: non-contact and contact types. </p><p>Non-contact CMMs utilize laser scanning or vision systems to measure objects without physical contact, offering speed and minimal risk of damage. They are ideal for delicate components. </p><p>In contrast, contact CMMs use mechanical probes to physically touch the object for precise measurements, ensuring high accuracy. They are suitable for various industrial applications where precise dimensional data is crucial. Each type serves specific measurement needs across industries.</p></p>
