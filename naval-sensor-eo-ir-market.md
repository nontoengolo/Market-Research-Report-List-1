<p><p>The naval sensor market, particularly in the EO-IR segment, comprises two main types: multispectral and hyperspectral sensors. Multispectral sensors capture a limited number of specific wavelengths across the electromagnetic spectrum, enabling essential applications such as surveillance and target detection. In contrast, hyperspectral sensors collect a broad range of wavelengths, allowing for more detailed spectral analysis and material identification. Both types enhance naval operational capabilities, providing critical intelligence and situational awareness in diverse maritime environments.</p></p>
