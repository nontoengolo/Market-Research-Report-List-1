In contrast, horizontal furnaces feature a conveyor system that facilitates continuous material flow, ideal for high-volume production. Both types serve the automotive sector by enhancing material properties, improving wear resistance, and extending the lifespan of critical vehicle components through advanced heat treatment processes.</p></p>
