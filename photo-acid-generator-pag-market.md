They generate acids upon exposure to UV light, facilitating chemical reactions in resists. The market is divided into ionic and non-ionic types. Ionic PAGs typically exhibit high solubility in polar solvents and provide stronger acid activation, while non-ionic PAGs offer better stability and reduced volatility, making them suitable for specific applications. Both types serve vital roles in enhancing resolution and performance in electronic devices.</p></p>
