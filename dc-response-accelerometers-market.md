<p><strong>The DC Response Accelerometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC response accelerometers are categorized mainly into two types: capacitive and piezo-resistive. Capacitive accelerometers operate on the principle of capacitance change due to acceleration, offering high sensitivity and stability for low-frequency measurements. They are ideal for applications such as structural health monitoring. Piezoresistive accelerometers utilize changes in electrical resistance due to mechanical stress, suitable for high-frequency and dynamic measurements. These types are widely used in automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics for precise motion detection and analysis.</p></p>
