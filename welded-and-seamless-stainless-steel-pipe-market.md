<p><p>The stainless steel pipe market comprises two primary types: welded and seamless. Welded stainless steel pipes are produced by welding together flat strips or plates, making them cost-effective and ideal for various applications. In contrast, seamless stainless steel pipes are manufactured from solid billets, ensuring uniformity and strength without weld seams, making them suitable for high-pressure environments. Each type serves distinct requirements in industries such as construction, oil and gas, and manufacturing, influenced by factors like pressure ratings and application needs.</p></p>
