<p><p>Wireless charging integrated circuits (ICs) are essential components in enabling efficient power transfer without physical connectors. The market is primarily divided into two types: transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs are responsible for converting electrical energy into electromagnetic waves, initiating the charging process. In contrast, receiver ICs capture these waves and convert them back into usable electrical energy for the device. Together, they facilitate various applications, driving growth in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
