<p><strong>The Scuba Diving Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Respiratory System</li><li>Closed Respiratory System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scuba diving equipment market is categorized into two main types: open respiratory systems and closed respiratory systems. Open respiratory systems allow divers to exhale air directly into the water, utilizing equipment like traditional scuba tanks and regulators. In contrast, closed respiratory systems recycle exhaled air, filtering and reintroducing it, enabling longer dive durations. These systems often include advanced technologies for enhanced safety and efficiency. Each type serves different needs, preferences, and diving environments, contributing to their unique appeal in the market.</p></p>
