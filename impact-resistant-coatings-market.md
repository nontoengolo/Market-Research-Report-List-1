Polyurethane-based coatings are known for their flexibility, durability, and UV resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring protection against abrasion and weathering. In contrast, epoxy-based coatings provide excellent adhesion, chemical resistance, and hardness, making them suitable for industrial and heavy-duty applications. Both types enhance surface longevity and integrity, offering protective solutions across various industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
