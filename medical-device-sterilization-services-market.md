<p><p>The medical device sterilization services market encompasses various methods to ensure the safety and efficacy of medical instruments. Ethylene Oxide (EtO) sterilization utilizes gas to penetrate and eliminate microorganisms at low temperatures, making it suitable for heat-sensitive devices. Steam sterilization uses high-pressure steam to provide effective microbial destruction and is commonly employed for reusable instruments. Electron beam sterilization employs high-energy radiation to achieve rapid sterilization, ideal for single-use devices. Other methods may include radiation and chemical sterilization techniques that cater to specific needs.</p></p>
