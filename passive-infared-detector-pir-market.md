<p><p>Passive Infrared Detectors (PIR) are sensors that detect motion by measuring changes in infrared radiation from objects, typically humans. The market for PIR devices is categorized into single-beam and multi-beam types. Single-beam PIRs focus on a single detection path, making them suitable for specific areas. In contrast, multi-beam PIRs utilize multiple detection paths, enhancing coverage and reducing false alarms, which is ideal for larger or complex spaces. Both types are widely used in security and automation applications.</p></p>
