<p><ul><li>Total Parenteral Solution</li><li>Peripheral Parenteral Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravenous (IV) solution market primarily consists of Total Parenteral Nutrition (TPN) and Peripheral Parenteral Nutrition (PPN). TPN delivers complete nutritional support directly into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system, making it essential for patients unable to consume food orally. In contrast, PPN provides partial nutritional support through a peripheral vein, usually for patients with less severe needs. Both types address specific patient requirements, shaping their use in clinical settings to ensure effective nutritional therapy.</p></p>
