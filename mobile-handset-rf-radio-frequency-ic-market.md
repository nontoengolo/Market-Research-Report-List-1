<p><p>The Mobile Handset RF IC market is categorized into three types: Analog, Digital, and Mixed Signal. Analog RF ICs handle continuous signals, essential for functions like signal amplification and modulation in mobile devices. Digital RF ICs process discrete signals, focusing on data transmission and processing, crucial for digital communication standards. Mixed Signal RF ICs combine both analog and digital functionalities, enabling efficient signal processing and improved performance in mobile handsets, which require seamless integration of various signal types for enhanced connectivity.</p></p>
