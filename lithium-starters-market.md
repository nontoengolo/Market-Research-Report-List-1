<p><p>Lithium starters in the market are primarily classified into two types: standalone and plug-in. Standalone lithium starters operate independently, providing reliable power for engines without needing external support. They are often used in applications where space and weight efficiency are crucial. In contrast, plug-in lithium starters can connect to external charging systems, allowing for enhanced flexibility and convenience. This type is typically favored in hybrid and electric vehicle applications, where seamless integration with existing power systems is essential.</p></p>
