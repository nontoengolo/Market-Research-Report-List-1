<p><p>Microgrid as a Service (MaaS) is a model that enables businesses and communities to access sophisticated microgrid solutions without significant upfront investment. There are primarily two market types: Grid-Tied Microgrids, which connect to the main power grid, allowing for energy exchange and backup support, enhancing reliability and efficiency; and Independent Microgrids, which operate autonomously, providing energy independence and resilience, particularly in remote or underserved areas. Both models emphasize sustainability, reduced operational costs, and adaptability to energy needs.</p></p>
